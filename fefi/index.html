Oliveira

Fefi Oliveira is a Latin actress, influencer, and singer. She is well known for her lead role on Nickelodeon’s TV show Club 57 (season 1 and 2) where she plays two characters: Mercedes and Delaila. Since 2012, she has done six sucessful soap operas for Telemundo and two TV Series for Nickelodeon. Miami has changed her life, and gave her the opportunity to participate, not only on these TV Series but, to be part of national TV commercials for well known brands such as At&t, Dr. Pepper, Pizza Hut, Toyota, Sedanos Supermarket, Orbit gum, Lyft and Bang Energy.

As a singer, Fefi has more than 4 singles with million of views on Youtube.
